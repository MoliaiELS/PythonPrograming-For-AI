### Answer

Here’s a suggested 4-day trip to Hong Kong:

**Day 1: Hong Kong Island Highlights**  
- Morning: Take the iconic Peak Tram to Victoria Peak for panoramic views of the city.  
- Afternoon: Explore Central and visit the Avenue of Stars in Tsim Sha Tsui (across the harbor).  
- Evening: Enjoy a Symphony of Lights cruise on Victoria Harbour.

**Day 2: Culture & Local Flavors**  
- Morning: Visit Man Mo Temple and explore Sheung Wan’s street markets.  
- Lunch: Try local favorites like dim sum at Tim Ho Wan or roast goose at Kam’s Roast Goose.  
- Afternoon: Stroll through PMQ, a creative hub in Central.  
- Evening: Sample street food in Mong Kok, such as egg waffles and fish balls.

**Day 3: Lantau Island Adventure**  
- Morning: Take the MTR to Lantau Island; ride the Ngong Ping 360 cable car to see the Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha).  
- Afternoon: Visit Po Lin Monastery and hike part of the Wisdom Path.  
- Evening: Return via Tung Chung and dine at Citygate Outlets.

**Day 4: Kowloon & Shopping**  
- Morning: Explore Temple Street Night Market (open early) and Jade Market.  
- Afternoon: Shop in Tsim Sha Tsui malls or visit the M+ Museum and West Kowloon Cultural District.  
- Evening: Dine with a view at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the harbor.

**Accommodation Tips:**  
Stay in Tsim Sha Tsui, Central, or Wan Chai—well-connected areas with great views and access to transit.

**Transportation:**  
Use the efficient MTR system, buses, trams, and ferries. Get an Octopus card for seamless travel across all modes.

Enjoy your trip to Hong Kong!
